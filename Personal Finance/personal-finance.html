Personal Money Manager

"A budget is telling your money where to go instead of wondering where it went." - Dave Ramsey

Description

The Personal Money Manager dashboard makes it easy to track and manage your finances. It provides a clear view of your income, expenses, and savings, helping you stay on top of your budget and reach your financial goals.

Key Features

  • Key Metrics: Income, Needs, Savings, Wants, Balance
  • Account Summary: Balance, Money In, Money Out
  • Spending Composition: Envelope System Percentage Breakdown
  • Cash Flow: Cumulative Cash on Hand
  • Monthly Analysis: Income and Expenses
  • Budget Comparison: 50-30-20 Rule (Planned vs Actual)
  • Financial Overview: Income vs. Expenses
  • Top Income Sources: Highlight of Top 5 Income Streams
  • Expense Breakdown: Detailed List of All Expenses by Subcategory

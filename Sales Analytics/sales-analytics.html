Sales Analytics

"You can't connect the dots looking forward. You can only connect them looking backward. So you have to trust that the dots will somehow connect in your future." - Steve Jobs

Description

The Sales Analytics Dashboard offers a comprehensive and detailed view of sales performance. Track key financial metrics, analyze operational data, and gain insights into sales trends, product performance, and customer demographics to drive informed business decisions.

Key Features

  • Financial Overview: Total Sales Revenue, Gross Profit, Gross Profit Margin %
  • Operational Metrics: Quantity Sold, Product Count, COGS
  • Sales Trends: Sales Revenue and Gross Profit History
  • Product Performance: Sales Revenue and Gross Profit by Category
  • Top Products: Top 5 Best-Selling Subcategories, Models, and Products
  • Top Customers: Top 5 Customers' Purchase Details and Sales Revenue
  • Customer Demographics: Sales Revenue by Gender and Age
  • Geographic Distribution: Sales Revenue by Country

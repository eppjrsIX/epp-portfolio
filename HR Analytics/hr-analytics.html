HR Analytics

"Human Resources isn't a thing we do. It's the thing that runs our business." - Steve Wynn

Description

The HR Analytics Dashboard offers a comprehensive and clear view of your workforce. Track key metrics, analyze trends, and gain insights into employee demographics and distributions to drive informed HR decisions.

Key Features (Employee Overview)

  • KPIs: Active Employees, Retention Rate, Turnover Rate, Satisfaction Rate
  • Yearly Trends in New Hires
  • Active Employee Distribution by Age Bracket
  • Comparison of Separated vs. Active Employees (including Percentage and Percentage Difference)
  • Active Employee Distribution by Department
  • Active Employee Distribution by Position Category
  • Active Employee Distribution by State
  • Gender Distribution of Active Employees
